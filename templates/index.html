What Is WebCrawler

It is a tool which crawls websites and report backs the gaps in adherence/compliance and also suggest ways to fix them.

IT IDENTIFIES THE FOLLOWING:

  1. 1. SSL certificate compliance – Check all links in the site for URL validation of SSL (all hyperlinks should be https://), and verify the validity of the SSL certificate.

  2. 2. Cookie checker – Verify cookies being used by the website, the cookie checker will scan the cookies on the website, and cookie consent verification links.

  3. 3. ADA compliance – Check websites for:
    ○ Alt text in all images.
    ○ Color contrast for the site as per w3.org guidelines.
    ○ Accessibility issues to check the site markup for null tab index
